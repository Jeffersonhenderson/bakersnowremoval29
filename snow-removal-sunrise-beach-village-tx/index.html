Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do I need to provide a space for you to deposit snow piles for disposal when you come for hauling?

Not necessarily. If you have space on your property where the snow can be stacked safely without blocking access, we’ll pile it there. But if you’d rather not keep large piles on-site, we’ll haul the snow off completely using trucks and dispose of it safely elsewhere.

2. How do you keep walkways from being damaged when moving your machines on them?

We’re very mindful of that. Our machines are equipped with rubber blades and pads that prevent scraping or cracking on delicate surfaces. For tight walkways or sensitive areas, our crew switches to shoveling or smaller tools to clear snow without causing any damage.

3. For how long will your anti-icing service protect snow or ice from forming on my surface?

It depends on the weather, but generally, our anti-icing treatment will last until the storm passes or until there’s heavy traffic that wears it off. What it does best is reduce the bond between snow/ice and the surface, making it easier and faster to clear when the time comes.

4. Do you remove snow from the roof?

Yes, we do. Roof snow can add extra weight and sometimes lead to leaks or even collapses if ignored. Our team safely clears snow from rooftops to prevent these risks while keeping safety measures in mind.

5. Can you specially offer snow shoveling service?

Yes, we can. Our snow shoveling service is available as a standalone option or as part of a complete snow removal plan.
